About Me

Malaz is a design student based in New York City studying at Parsons School of Design. She has an interest in coding and building things, which started her fascination with understanding what is possible in bridging tech and design. In her free time, she enjoys cooking, shopping, and hanging out with her friends. In the future, she hopes to work in a community where she is able to constantly create new things to help bring joy into the world!
